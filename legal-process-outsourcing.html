Services we provide

Our skilled legal team delivers comprehensive compliance and expert contract services, keeping your business protected and ahead of regulatory challenges, while you focus on growth.

Contract Review and Drafting

●We review, draft, and negotiate contracts and agreements to safeguard your company’s interests.
●Our legal experts ensure full compliance with laws and regulations while identifying and mitigating any risks.

Compliance and Regulatory Oversight

●VJC assists in developing compliance frameworks to align your business operations with required legal mandates.
●We ensure compliance by staying up to date with regulations in the client’s country, as well as international practices that directly or indirectly impact the client’s trade or profession.

Legal Research and Analysis

●Your team conducts comprehensive legal research, offering detailed insights into relevant laws, regulations, and precedents.
●We provide advisory services on the legal implications of business decisions and offer strategies for risk mitigation.

Litigation Support

●Our litigation support services include document review, evidence gathering, and case preparation.
●We also collaborate with external legal counsel to represent your company’s interests in legal disputes.

Intellectual Property (IP) Management

●VJC provides strategic legal support for protecting and managing intellectual property assets, including trademarks, patents, and copyrights.
●We assist in drafting and reviewing IP agreements, licensing, and enforcement actions to safeguard your intellectual property rights.

Mergers and Acquisitions Due Diligence

●We conduct thorough legal due diligence to identify potential risks and liabilities in mergers, acquisitions, and business transactions.
●Our team provides comprehensive assessments of contractual obligations, compliance status, and regulatory adherence before finalizing deals.

Training and Awareness

●We offer tailored legal training programs to help your team understand key regulations, compliance issues, and company policies.
●Our sessions foster a culture of ethical conduct and legal awareness across your organization.

Data Breach and GDPR Compliance

●We assist businesses in developing data protection policies and ensuring adherence to global data privacy regulations, including GDPR.
●We conduct thorough compliance checks and help implement necessary security measures to prevent data breaches.
