Year-end legal compliance is thoroughly checked, and ledgers are reviewed to identify accounting errors. We also inspect key entries like deferred and unbilled revenue and prepaid expenses. Statutory Audit Support

Comprehensive planning, execution, and compliance to ensure accurate financial statements, audit readiness, and regulatory adherence. ...read more

We offer a comprehensive statutory audit support process, covering planning, execution, and finalization.

1. Planning: Prepare a requirement list, review prior audits, verify opening balances, select samples, and calculate materiality.

2. Execution: Prepare and review working papers, test internal controls, gather and verify evidence, summarize audit observations, perform casting procedures, assess risks, and assist with balance confirmations.

3. Finalization: Review and prepare financial statements, ensure audit file completeness, draft management letters, develop internal control procedures, check compliance with standards, and draft notes for proper disclosures. ...collapse
