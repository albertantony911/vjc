Services we provide

We offer precise, secure bookkeeping for startups and established companies, allowing you to focus on growth. Our tailored audit and assurance services build investor confidence and help businesses of all sizes stay ahead of regulatory challenges with comprehensive support.

Pre-Audit Review

Pre-audit comprehensive review and validation of financial statements to ensure accuracy, compliance, and reliability; Perform cut-off procedures to ensure that all supplier invoices, sales invoices, expenses, etc. are recorded appropriately; Ensuring that all year-end procedures including legal compliance are properly complied with; Identification of accounting errors through detailed ledgers scrutiny; Inspection of all entries related to deferred revenue, unbilled revenue, prepaid expenses and others; Finalizing and providing a draft set of financial statements after considering the adjustments identified during the pre-audit review.

Internal Audits

Partner with us for Internal Auditing: Enhance Corporate Performance and Governance Internal auditing plays a crucial role in assessing and fortifying a company's internal control systems, corporate governance structures, and adherence to legal and regulatory requirements. Its primary goal is to ensure that financial reporting is both accurate and timely, to boost operational efficiency, and to protect company assets.

We understand that each company is unique, and therefore, our internal audit approach is tailored to the specific scope of work and goals of the audit. Our internal audit services are designed to assist executives and board members in efficiently managing performance, assessing risks, and improving employee productivity. We focus on monitoring the firms financial health while aiming to enhance overall efficiency, effectiveness, and quality within the organization.

Statutory Audit Support

Comprehensive planning, execution, and compliance to ensure accurate financial statements, audit readiness, and regulatory adherence. We offer a comprehensive statutory audit support process, covering planning, execution, and finalization.

We offer a comprehensive statutory audit support process, covering planning, execution, and finalization.

1. Planning: Prepare a requirement list, review prior audits, verify opening balances, select samples, and calculate materiality.

2. Execution: Prepare and review working papers, test internal controls, gather and verify evidence, summarize audit observations, perform casting procedures, assess risks, and assist with balance confirmations.

3. Finalization: Review and prepare financial statements, ensure audit file completeness, draft management letters, develop internal control procedures, check compliance with standards, and draft notes for proper disclosures.
