Beyond the work

For low-income patients

Our founder Vijay Jaiswal had a cardiac event in his thirties. He recovered. Many from low-income communities in India do not because the treatment they need is out of financial reach. We fund and subsidise cardiac diagnosis and treatment for low-income patients through Narayana Hrudayalaya. Vijay leads this work personally.

For marginalised women

We partner with NGOs across India to provide skills training, education support and employment opportunities for women from marginalised communities including pathways into finance and operations roles at VJC. Mayank Berlia leads the program.

For accounting & tax students

Prachi Priya and Deep Shikha run a mentoring and counselling program for accounting and tax students including career direction, technical preparation, and practical questions about the profession that do not get answered in lecture halls.
