Solutions We Provide

SMSF Administration Support

VJC supports day-to-day SMSF administration, including document collection, transaction review, member record updates and file organisation. We keep the fund file structured so your team can review the position without chasing scattered records.

SMSF Accounting & Workpapers

Our team prepares SMSF accounts, reconciliations and workpapers across bank accounts, investment accounts, income, expenses, member balances and year-end adjustments. Workpapers are prepared in your format or VJC’s structured Excel-based format.

Investment & Income Reconciliation

VJC assists with reconciliation of bank accounts, managed funds, shares, dividends, distributions, interest income and other investment records. Supporting schedules are prepared so investment income and balances can be reviewed clearly.

Member Balances & Pension Support

We assist with member balance updates, contribution tracking, pension payment checks and related support schedules. The aim is to keep member-level records clean and aligned with the fund’s accounting file.

Audit Pack & Query Support

VJC prepares organised audit support packs, including workpapers, reconciliations, source documents and query notes. We also assist with audit query responses where your firm wants support during the SMSF audit process.

Annual Return Support

Our team supports preparation of SMSF Annual Return workpapers and related schedules for your firm’s review. Your registered tax agent remains responsible for final review, approval and lodgement.

VJC's Smartsourcing Approach

SMSF work needs clean records, strong document trails and careful attention to member-level details.

VJC’s Smartsourcing approach focuses on organised workpapers, completed reconciliations, clear audit support and early query identification. Your team stays in control of technical review, client advice and lodgement. We prepare the file so it can move through review and audit with less back-and-forth.
