1988

37 year old legacy

Established in 1988, VJC is an Indian certified public accounting firm set up in the quaint town of Ambikapur, Chhattisgarh. With a vision to support, guide, and encourage businesses in the area, the firm has been an indivisible partner to all its clients through the years. We are driven by a commitment to excellence and a relentless pursuit of innovation.

NOW

Where it stands now

Our mission is to simplify your financial processes, serving as a virtual extension of your accounting department. Our blended shore model ensures you receive consistent, high-quality service around the clock, leveraging the expertise of our global team. We dont just provide services; we offer peace of mind, knowing your financial operations are in capable hands. We strive to help businesses of all sizes save a large percentage of their processing expenses.

At VJC India, we believe client satisfaction to be paramount. Join us at VJC, where we strive to elevate accounting from a hassle to a strategic advantage for your business.

Our infrastructure

Security and Safety

We ensure robust data security through advanced protocols across networks, devices, and communications. Mobile management, encrypted transmissions, secure VPNs, and firewall protection safeguard data integrity. Adhering to GDPR and security standards, we conduct audits, risk assessments, and maintain a standardized environment to uphold data privacy and operational security.

Operational Management

Our operations use high-performance Dell/HP workstations with advanced CPUs, dual monitors, and SSD storage, ensuring productivity and reliability. Secure virtual desktops enable seamless remote access, while proactive IT monitoring ensures efficiency. Regular updates and robust disaster recovery guarantee continuity, exemplifying our commitment to optimized, resilient workflows.

Physical Infrastructure

Our facility embodies sophistication and security with ergonomic workstations, 24/7 HD CCTV surveillance, and biometric access. Advanced fire safety systems, UPS support, and diesel generators ensure uninterrupted operations. Modern meeting rooms, relaxation areas, and a cafeteria create a productive, secure environment, reflecting our commitment to reliable, high-quality service delivery.
