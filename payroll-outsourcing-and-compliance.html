Services we provide

Core Payroll Processing

VJC supports weekly, fortnightly and monthly pay cycle preparation across wages, salaries, allowances, deductions, reimbursements and contractor payments. Pay runs are prepared inside your client’s payroll software with payroll reports, payslips and working notes organised for review.

Employee & Contractor Setup

Our team assists with employee onboarding, Tax File Number (TFN) declaration tracking, super fund details, employment classifications and contractor setup. This includes Australian Business Number (ABN) checks, PAYG withholding review for contractors and working holiday maker setup for subclass 417 and 462 employees.

STP, PAYG & ATO Reporting Support

VJC supports STP pay event preparation, STP update events, correction support and year-end finalisation assistance through STP-enabled software. We also reconcile PAYG withholding amounts to payroll reports and Business Activity Statement (BAS) workpapers before review.

Superannuation & Payday Super Readiness

We assist with Superannuation Guarantee (SG) calculation checks, ordinary time earnings review, super payable reconciliations and clearing house workflows. With Payday Super applying from 1 July 2026, we help firms prepare cleaner payroll processes, fund data checks and payment timing controls.

Leave, Awards & Termination Support

Payroll errors often sit in leave accruals, award treatment, allowances, penalties and final pay calculations. VJC supports annual leave, personal leave, long service leave, termination pay, unused leave and redundancy calculation workpapers for review.

Payroll Compliance & Reporting

VJC prepares payroll-linked compliance support schedules, including PAYG withholding reconciliations, Taxable Payments Annual Report (TPAR) support, Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT) payroll workpapers and payroll tax threshold assessment support. We also assist with payroll clearing account reviews and month-end payroll reporting.

Your firm stays in control of approval and client relationships. We reduce the processing load and help keep payroll files review-ready.
