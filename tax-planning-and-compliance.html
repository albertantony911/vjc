At VJC, we provide comprehensive outsourced tax preparation services for various entities in the U.S., including:

  • Individuals: Form 1040, Form 1040NR, Form 1040A, Form 1040EZ
  • Partnerships: Form 1065
  • Corporations: Form 1120, Form 1120A, Form 1120S (for statutory corporations)
  • Non-Profit Organizations: Form 990
  • Fiduciaries: Form 1041
  • Estate & Gift Tax: Form 706
  • Payroll Tax: Form 940, Form 941, Form 944, Form W-2
  • Sales & Use Tax: State-specific Sales & Use Tax Returns
  • Year-End Reporting: Form 1099 series

We ensure accurate, timely filing for all types of returns, helping you stay compliant with U.S. tax regulations.


At VJC, we offer comprehensive tax planning and compliance services for Australian businesses and individuals, including the preparation of Business Activity Statements (BAS) and related workpapers. Our services cover various taxes based on your business size, employee count, benefits offered, and location. We assist with:

  • Goods and Services Tax (GST)
  • Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT)
  • Pay as You Go Instalments (PAYGI)
  • Pay as You Go Withholding (PAYGW)
  • Fuel Tax Credits

We ensure your income tax return and fringe benefits tax return are lodged accurately and on time with the Australian Taxation Office (ATO), helping you avoid penalties for late lodgment. Our services also include:

  • Accurate preparation of tax filings
  • Preparation of financial statements
  • Stress-free compliance, reducing the burden of managing taxes yourself

Additionally, we offer international tax advisory for Australian entities and strategic tax planning to minimize tax liabilities and maximize savings for both individuals and corporations.


We offer comprehensive tax services to meet the varying needs of UK-based businesses and individuals, ensuring compliance with HMRC requirements. Our services include:

  • Self-Assessment Tax Returns: Accurate preparation and timely submission of self-assessment returns, ensuring all applicable reliefs and deductions are claimed.
  • VAT Returns: Assistance with VAT registration and preparation of VAT returns, ensuring compliance with the latest regulations.
  • PAYE and Payroll Services: Management of PAYE, including real-time information (RTI) submissions and payroll processing.
  • Capital Gains Tax: Accurate filing and reporting of capital gains on property, stocks, or other assets, ensuring minimization of tax liabilities through applicable reliefs.
  • Corporate Tax: End-to-end preparation and filing of corporate tax returns for UK-based companies, ensuring compliance with local legislation.
  • International Tax Advisory: Strategic planning and advisory services to help businesses manage their cross-border tax obligations, minimizing global tax liabilities.

By working with us, UK clients can ensure that all submissions are made on time, reducing stress and avoiding penalties from HMRC. Additionally, our tax planning expertise helps to optimize tax outcomes for both individuals and businesses.
